About us

The Birla Institute of Technology (BIT) Extension Centre in Deoghar is a prominent educational institution located in Deoghar, Jharkhand, India. It serves as an extension of the renowned Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, Ranchi. The center offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines, including engineering, management, and computer applications.


It provides a conducive learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty members. The BIT Extension Centre in Deoghar aims to impart quality education and foster academic excellence, nurturing students' skills and talents to prepare them for successful careers in their respective fields. It also contributes to the socio-economic development of the region by promoting education and research.

Department

  • Department of Computer Science and Engineering

    This department focuses on the study and development of computer systems and software. It offers courses in programming, algorithms, database management, artificial intelligence, and computer networks.

  • Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering

    This department deals with the study of electrical systems, electronics, and their applications. Students learn about power systems, electrical machines, control systems, electronic devices, and circuit design.

  • Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

    This department focuses on electronic devices, circuits, and communication systems. Students learn about electronic circuits, signal processing, wireless communication, digital communication, and microwave engineering.

  • Department of Mechanical Engineering

    The department of Mechanical Engineering emphasizes the study of machines, mechanics, and manufacturing processes. Students learn about thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, solid mechanics, materials science, and design engineering.

  • Department of Civil Engineering

    This department deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects. Students learn about structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, environmental engineering, and project management.

  • Department of Chemical Engineering

    The department of Chemical Engineering focuses on the study of chemical processes, reactions, and plant operations. Students learn about chemical kinetics, thermodynamics, process control, separation processes, and chemical plant design.

  • Department of Production Engineering

    This department emphasizes the study of manufacturing processes, production systems, and industrial automation. Students learn about machine tools, materials handling, production planning, quality control, and supply chain management.

  • Department of Information Technology

    This department focuses on the study and application of information systems and technology in various domains. Students learn about software development, database management, web technologies, network security, and data analytics.

  • Department of Biotechnology

    The department of Biotechnology deals with the application of biological processes and organisms in various fields. Students learn about genetic engineering, bioprocess engineering, bioinformatics, biomedical engineering, and pharmaceutical technology.

  • Department of Management Studies

    This department offers courses in management and business administration. Students learn about organizational behavior, marketing management, financial management, human resource management, and strategic management.
